# Contributor Covenant Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We pledge to make participation in this project a harassment-free experience for everyone.

## Our Standards

Expected behavior:
- respectful, constructive technical collaboration
- clear and honest feedback
- accountability for mistakes and corrections

Unacceptable behavior:
- harassment, discrimination, personal attacks
- doxxing or publication of private information without permission
- abusive or threatening conduct in public or private channels

## Enforcement

Project maintainers are responsible for enforcement.
Report incidents to: `conduct@yai.local` (placeholder).
All reports are reviewed promptly and handled confidentially.
